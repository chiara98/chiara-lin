About

I grew up creating attacks on the soccer field, choking companions into submission on the Jiu-Jitsu mat, and getting tangled in plots woven by crafty fiction writers. I dreamed of traveling to see the world and helping people as a doctor. My passion for helping others has remained. I'm someone who has heart and will work hard to achieve my goals. I can’t tell you that I grew up being passionate about computers and knew that I wanted to go into the tech field. I grew up actually wanting to do the complete opposite. But the one thing that didn’t change from my childhood was my purpose. I had a bleeding heart for the world and that is something that I held on to. The only thing that changed is in what tools I want to use.

I discovered my love for computer science and its relation to cognitive science after my first year in college. I knew computer science was something I loved as I could code for hours and not feel time slipping by. I am passionate about using those disciplines as tools to create a better world for someone. Being able to see the connection between machines and humans and creating solutions that will not only improve society as a whole but also have the ability to impact people on an individual level is something that I want to apply myself to. My passion is what I use and will continue to use as fuel for my dreams and goals.
